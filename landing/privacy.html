Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

CashFlow AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our cash flow forecasting platform.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account information (name, email, company details)
  • Financial data from connected bank accounts via Plaid
  • Transaction history for cash flow analysis
  • Usage data and analytics

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our cash flow forecasting services
  • Generate AI-powered predictions and insights
  • Send alerts and notifications about your cash position
  • Improve our algorithms and user experience
  • Communicate with you about your account

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • 256-bit AES encryption for all data at rest
  • TLS 1.3 encryption for data in transit
  • SOC 2 Type II certified infrastructure
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Multi-factor authentication support

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to provide our platform:

  • Plaid for secure bank account connections
  • Stripe for payment processing
  • Cloud infrastructure providers (AWS/GCP)

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting support.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt out of marketing communications
